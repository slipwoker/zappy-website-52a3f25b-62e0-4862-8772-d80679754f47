Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before bringing in your device — straight answers, no runaround.

01

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs — including screen replacements, battery swaps, and charging port fixes — are completed the same day, often within 1–2 hours. More complex issues like motherboard repairs or water damage may take 24–48 hours. We'll always give you a realistic timeframe upfront so there are no surprises.

02

Is my personal data safe during the repair?

Absolutely. We treat your data with full respect and confidentiality. Our technicians work only on the hardware components needed for the repair and do not access your files, photos, or messages. We recommend backing up your device beforehand as a precaution, but your privacy is always protected.

03

Do you provide a quote before starting any work?

Yes, always. We diagnose your device first and give you a clear, honest price quote before any repair work begins. You decide whether to proceed — no hidden fees, no pressure, no surprises on the bill. That's the transparent service standard we hold ourselves to at בית הנייד והמחשב.

04

Can you replace screens and batteries on all phone models?

Yes. We carry replacement screens and batteries for a wide range of smartphones including iPhone, Samsung, Xiaomi, Huawei, and more. Whether it's a cracked display or a battery that no longer holds a charge, our lab handles screen and battery replacements for most popular models quickly and at fair prices.

05

Do you repair gaming consoles too?

Yes! Our repair lab handles gaming consoles including PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch. Common issues we fix include HDMI port damage, overheating, disk drive failures, and controller problems. Bring it in for a free diagnosis and we'll let you know exactly what it needs.

06

Do you sell accessories in-store?

Definitely. Our store carries a full range of accessories including phone cases, screen protectors, chargers, cables, headphones, speakers, and more. Whether you need something to protect your device or upgrade your setup, you'll find it right here — no need to order online and wait.

Before You Visit

Get the Most Out of Your Repair Visit

A little preparation goes a long way. Follow these quick tips before bringing in your device — so we can diagnose faster, fix smarter, and save you money.

Important

Back Up Your Data First

Before any repair, back up your photos, contacts, and files to the cloud or a computer. While we handle every device with care, some repairs (especially screen replacements or motherboard work) may require a factory reset. Don't risk losing your memories.

Use iCloud, Google Drive, or a USB backup
Helpful

Bring Your Charger & Accessories

If your issue involves charging, audio, or connectivity, bring the original charger, cable, or accessory. This helps our technicians reproduce the problem accurately and avoid unnecessary part replacements — keeping your repair cost as low as possible.

Original accessories speed up diagnosis significantly
Important

Describe the Symptoms Clearly

When did it start? What were you doing when it happened? Does it occur every time or occasionally? The more detail you give, the faster we can pinpoint the problem. Think of it like visiting a doctor — a clear description saves time and money for everyone.

Note any recent drops, spills, or software updates
Pro Tip

Always Ask for the Most Cost-Effective Option

At בית הנייד והמחשב, we always offer honest advice — including when it's not worth repairing. Don't hesitate to ask: "What's the most affordable fix?" or "Is it worth repairing vs. replacing?" Our goal is to help you make the smartest decision for your budget, not upsell you on parts you don't need.

We offer free diagnostic estimates — no obligation to proceed
